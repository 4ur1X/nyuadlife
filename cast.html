RONIT SINGH


Ronit is earning a degree in Interactive Media from New York University Abu Dhabi. Following his passion for film, storytelling and adventure, he went to work in television and documentary filmmaking.

He recently finished his first feature length documentary and won the 2022 Audience Choice Award at New York Film Festival and the Paris Film Festival.

Film Director, NFA (NYUAD FILM ACADEMY)



DHURBA TRIPATHI


Dhurba is a professional stop-motion animator with over 5 years of experience. He mainly works on children's TV series, but likes to make his own independent films whenever he gets the chance.

He is a Nepal native, has previously directed two award-winning short films and is currently in development on a feature film, that he wrote and will direct.

Film Producer, NFA (NYUAD FILM ACADEMY)
